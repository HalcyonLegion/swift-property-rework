Auction Property & Conveyancing Solicitors

BBS Law - Auction Property & Conveyancing Solicitors

BBS Law has the expertise and knowledge to assist buyers and sellers at auction with confidence. Our highly regarded property team has vast experience with handling sales, purchases and where required finance including bridging finance. Our team understands the pressures that the process may give rise to and are able to provide expert advice to you on any potential risks or legal requirements that you may not be aware of.

Buying & Selling Property At Auction

At BBS Law we are well versed in appreciating the complexities and challenges that come with acting for buyers and sellers of residential property, commercial and mixed use property, Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs) and the sale of land as well as other different types of assets and we can assist you to ensure you are following the correct procedures and are adequately protected.
